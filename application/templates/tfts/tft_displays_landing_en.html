TFT Graphic

To communicate information and to manage datas, colour pictures and high rendering TFT displays are very fashionables. We have the following sizes: from 2.4" to 15" or more. The available resolutions may vary from 160 x 240 to 1024 x 768. For reading the text, the sense of reading may be in Landscape or Portrait versions. For the Backlight type we dispose of different techniques: LED, CCFL. We dispose of all kinds of Digital or Analog TFT from our standard lists and of special dedicated solutions with broad temperature range, high luminosity, integrated touch panel, for example.
